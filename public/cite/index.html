Research Using Occlu-FER#

If you have published research using the Occlu-FER dataset, please let us know! We’d be happy to include your work in our list of related publications.

How to Submit Your Paper#

Send us an email at your-email@university.edu with:

  • Paper title and authors
  • Publication venue and year
  • Link to the paper (if available)
  • Brief description of how you used the dataset

Citation Guidelines#

When citing the Occlu-FER dataset, please:

  1. Use the complete citation including all author names and publication details
  2. Mention the dataset name explicitly in your paper
  3. Include the dataset URL in your references: https://your-username.github.io/Occlu-FER/
  4. Acknowledge the contribution of the dataset to your research
  5. Follow the terms of use as specified in our download page

Dataset Statistics for Citation#

When describing the dataset in your paper, you may use these statistics:

  • Total samples: [Number] images/videos
  • Expression categories: 7 basic emotions
  • Occlusion types: [Number] different occlusion patterns
  • Subjects: [Number] individuals
  • Collection period: [Time period]
  • Annotation method: [Description of annotation process]

Acknowledgments#

If you use this dataset, please also consider acknowledging:

  • The research team and institution
  • Funding sources that supported the dataset creation
  • Contributors who helped with data collection and annotation
  • The broader research community working on facial expression recognition

Contact for Citation Issues#

If you have questions about proper citation or need additional information for your references, please contact us at your-email@university.edu.

Thank you for properly citing our work! Your citations help us demonstrate the impact of our research and secure continued funding for dataset development.
